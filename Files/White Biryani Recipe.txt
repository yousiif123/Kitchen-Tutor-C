16 White Biryani Recipe

Ingredients for stock:
800g Chicken
1/4 cup Onion (sliced)
1 tbsp Ginger (cubes)
4 Green cardamoms
4 Black cardamoms
4 Star anises
1 tbsp Cumin seeds
1 tsp Black peppercorn
2 Bay leaves
Ingredients for gravy:
1/2 cup Onion (sliced)
2 tbsp Oil
1 cup Yogurt
1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
1 tsp Cumin seeds
1 tsp Coriander powder
1 tbsp White pepper powder
Salt to taste
2-3 Green chilies 1 tsp Cumin powder
Ingredients for assembling:
1 cup Rice (boiled)
2 tbsp Coriander (chopped)
1/2 cup Stock
Directions:
In a pan add 2 cups water, chicken, boil it and remove them skim.
Then add green cardamom, black cardamom, star anise, cumin seeds, black pepper, bay leaf, onion, ginger and cook on low flame for 15 minutes.
Your stock is ready, strain and set aside.
In a pan heat oil add onion, cumin seeds, green chilies, ginger garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
Then add chicken, yogurt, coriander powder, cumin powder, white pepper, salt and cook for 5 to 7 minutes. Your gravy is ready.
Then in a pan layer gravy, boiled rice and repeat the layers.
Add half cup chicken stock, sprinkle coriander on top and let it simmer for 10 to 12 miutes.
Your biryani is ready to serve.
