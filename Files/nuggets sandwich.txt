12 Ingredients for nuggets sandwich:

1 Bread (sandwich)
10 K&N's Nuggets
2 tbsp Black olives (sliced)
1/2 cup Ice burg lettuce (chopped)
1/4 cup Sweet corns
1/4 cup Jalapeno (sliced)
1/4 cup Cucumber (sliced) 1/4 cup Onion (sliced)
2-3 Tomatoes slices
Cheese slices as required
Ingredients for dynamite sauce:
3/4 cup Mayonnaise
2 tsp Lemon Juice
5 tbsp Sriracha sauce
1 tsp Honey
Ingredients for honey mustard:
1/4 cup Honey
1/4 cup Mayonnaise
1 tbsp Dijon mustard
2 tbsp Lemon juice
1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
Directions for dynamite sauce:
In a bowl add sriracha sauce, lemon juice, honey and mayonnaise.
Mix them well and your dynamite sauce is ready. Set aside.
Directions for honey mustard sauce:
In a bowl add honey, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, lemon juice and cayenne pepper.
Mix all these ingredients well and your honey mustard sauce is ready. Set aside.
Assembling:
Prepare K&N's Nuggets as per the instructions on pack.
Take bread; add olives, jalapeno, ice burg lettuce, tomato slices, sweet corns, cucumbers and onions.
Top it with K&N's Nuggets, drizzle honey mustard and dynamite sauce on top.
Add cheese slices and toast it in oven for 3 to 4 minutes or until cheese melts.
Serve and enjoy your nuggets sandwich.
