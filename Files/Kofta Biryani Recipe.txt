17 Kofta Biryani Recipe
Ingredients

Beef Mince 500g
Rice 2 glasses
Salt as per taste
Onion (chopped) 1
Mint (chopped) 5g
Coriander (chopped) 5g
Green Chili 2
Poppy Seeds 1-1/2 tbsp
Roasted Chickpeas Powder 3 tbsp
Ginger Paste 1 tbsp
Turmeric 1 tsp
Red Chili Powder 2 tbsp
Red Chili Crushed 1 tsp
Coriander Powder 1 tbsp
Black Pepper Powder 1 tsp
Cooking Oil 1 cup
Ginger Garlic Paste 1-1/2 tsp
Tomato Paste 6 tomatoes
Yogurt 1/2 cup
Cumin Powder 1 tbsp
Garnish Tomato, Green Chili, Green Coriander
Garam Masala 1 tsp
Water 2 glasses
Yellow Color 1/2 tsp
Cooking Method:

1- In a bowl, add beef mince, onion, mint, coriander, green chili, poppy seeds, ginger paste, turmeric, red chili powder (1 tbsp), red chili crushed (1/2 tbsp), cumin powder (1/2 tbsp), coriander powder (1/2 tbsp), black pepper powder (1/2 tsp) and mix it well.

2- Now make the meatballs.

3- In a cooking pan, add cooking oil (1/2 cup) and shallow fry the meatballs.

4- In a cooking pan, cooking oil (3 tbsp), ginger garlic paste and cook for a while.

5- Now, add tomato paste, yogurt, red chili powder (1 tbsp), red chili crushed (1/2 tbsp), black pepper powder (1/2 tsp), coriander powder (1/2 tbsp), cumin powder (1/2 tbsp) and cook for 2-3 minutes.

6- Now, add meatballs, garnish and cook for a while.

7- Now, steam for 2-3 minutes and then cook 2-3 minutes.

8- In a cooking pan, add cooking oil (3 tbsp), garam masala, salt, garnish, water and rice and cook for 2-3 minutes or cook until water remain in little quantity.

9- Now, add garnish, meat balls, yellow color and simmer for 8-10 minutes.

Your Kofta Biryani now ready to serve.
