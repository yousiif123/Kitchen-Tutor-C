24 Ingredients for schezwan chicken

600 gm Chicken Boneless

1 & 1/2 tbsp Vinegar
6 tbsp Corn flour
1 tsp Black Pepper Powder
1 Egg
Cooking oil (as needed)
1 tbsp Garlic paste
1 tbsp Ginger Paste
4 Whole Red Chili
3 Green Chili
1 & 1/2 tbsp Chili Paste
Salt (as needed)
2 tbsp Soy Sauce
4 tbsp Ketchup
1 tbsp Sugar
¼ tsp Red Food Color
½ cup Chicken Stock
Instructions for schezwan chicken:

1- In a bowl, add chicken, salt, vinegar, corn flour, soy sauce, black pepper powder, egg and mix it well.

2- Keep in the refrigerator for an hour. After that fry it in the cooking oil until golden brown.
3- In a cooking pan, add cooking , garlic paste, ginger paste, whole red chili, green chili and cook for 2 minutes.
4- Now add, chili paste, fried chicken, salt, soy sauce, ketchup, sugar, vinegar, black pepper powder and cook for 2-3 minutes.
5- Now add, chicken stock, corn flour (mixed with water) and cook until thickend.
6- Your tasty Schezwan now ready Serve with Boiled Rice.
