
3 beef marination:


1 kg Beef (cut into strips)
1/2 cup Mustard oil
1/2 cup Yogurt
1 tsp Cumin powder
Salt to taste
1/2 tsp Carom seeds
1/4 tsp Yellow food color
1 tbsp Red chili powder
1 tsp Red chili flakes
1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
2 tbsp Lemon juice
Ingredients for gravy:
2 tbsp Oil
1 Onion (chopped)
1 cup Tomato puree
1 cup Yogurt
1 tbsp Red chili powder
1 tsp Cumin seeds
1-2 Green chilies (chopped)
1 tsp Coriander powder
1 tsp Garam masala
1/4 cup Brown onion paste
1 tsp Green chili paste
1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
Salt to taste
Directions:
In a bowl mix mustard oil, yogurt, cumin powder, salt, lemon juice, ginger garlic paste, carom seeds, red chili powder, chili flakes, food color and mix well.
Add beef strips in it and marinate for 4 hours or overnight. Keep in refrigerator.
Take wooden or steel skewers and thread beef on it. Cook on charcoal or pan until 50% cooked.
In pan heat oil, add onion, cumin seeds, green chilies and sauté for a minute.
Add ginger garlic paste, brown onion, tomato puree, yogurt, salt, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, green chili paste and cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
Add cooked bihari kabab, mix well, add hot water half cup and steam for 8 to 10 minutes.
Your bihari kabab masala is ready.
