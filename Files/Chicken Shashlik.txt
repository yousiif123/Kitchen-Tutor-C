22 Chicken Shashlik
Ingredients

Chicken Boneless (cube cutting) 300g
Rice 300g
Salt as per taste
Red Chili Crushed ½ tsp
Black Pepper, Crushed ½ tsp
Sugar ½ tsp
Cooking Oil 1 ½ tbsp
Ginger & Garlic Paste 1 tbsp
Capsicum 2 pieces
Onion 1 piece
Tomato 1 piece
Tomato Paste 1 tsp
Chili Garlic Sauce 1tsp
Tomato Ketchup 1 tbsp
Soy Sauce 1 tbsp
Carrots 1 piece
Cabbage 10 g
Egg 2 pieces
Corn Flour 2 tbsp
Chicken Stock ½ cup
Method

1-In a pot, add and marinate chicken, egg, salt, green chili, black pepper, sugar.
Now keep in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
2-In cooking pot, add cooking oil, ginger & garlic paste, marinated chicken and cook for 2-3 minutes.
3-Now add, salt, tomato ketchup, soy sauce, tomato paste, chili sauce and mix it.
4-Afterward, add capsicum, onion, tomato and cook for 2-3 minutes and add chicken stock, and corn flour mixed with water. Now cook for 2-3 minutes.
Egg Fried Rice Cooking Direction
In a cooking pot add, cooking oil, egg, capsicum, cabbage, carrots, boiled rice, salt, black pepper, soy sauce, green onion and cook for a while.
Your tasty Chicken Shashlik With Egg Fried Rice is ready to serve.
