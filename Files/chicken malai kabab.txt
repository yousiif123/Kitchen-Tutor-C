5 chicken malai kabab:

1kg Chicken mince
2 & 1/2 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
2 tbsp White pepper powder
1 tbsp Green chili paste
1 tbsp Mint
1 tbsp Green coriander
1 tsp Cumin powder
1 tsp Coriander powder
3 tbsp Khoya
Salt to taste
250g Fat
200g Mozzarella cheese
3 tbsp Cashew nut paste
Ingredients for gravy:
3 tbsp Oil
1/2 cup Milk
1/2 cup Yogurt
3 tbsp Cream
1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
1 tbsp Cashew nut paste
1 tbsp Almond paste
1 tbsp White pepper powder
1/2 cup Onion (chopped)
1 tbsp Cumin seeds
Directions for kabab:
In a bowl add chicken mince, fat, salt, white pepper, ginger garlic paste, green chili paste, cumin powder, coriander powder, khoya and cashew nut paste.
Mix all these ingredients well and keep in refrigerator for 2 hours.
In last add mozzarella cheese and mix well again. Take skewers and thread kabab on them.
Cook on charcoal until fully cooked.
Directions for gravy:
In a pan heat oil, add onion, ginger garlic paste, cumin seeds and cook until translucent.
Then add salt, yogurt, cashew nut paste, and cook for 5 minutes.
Then add milk, prepared kabab cover and cook for 5 more minutes.
In last add cream mix well and your dish is ready.
